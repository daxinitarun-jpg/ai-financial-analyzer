About FinSight AI

FinSight AI is a cutting-edge financial analysis tool designed to bridge the gap between dense corporate annual reports and actionable insights. Built for investors, analysts, and students, it leverages advanced natural language processing to extract quantitative data from unstructured PDFs.

By connecting to Google's Gemini 2.0 Flash AI, the platform intelligently interprets financial jargon, computes critical ratios (like ROE and Debt-to-Equity), and assesses macroeconomic and company-specific risks—all within seconds.

Best of all, your data stays private. Using zero-backend LZ-string compression, you can instantly share your analysis dashboard with colleagues via a simple URL link, without requiring any database or login.
